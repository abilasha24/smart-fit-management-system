Weight Loss (Beginner)
High-protein • low sugar • daily steps
Goal: Fat loss (small calorie deficit) Breakfast: Eggs + fruit Lunch: Rice (small) + dhal + chicken/fish + salad Snack: Yogurt / nuts Dinner: Vegetables + protein Water: 2–3L • Walk: 8k steps • Sleep: 7–8h
Muscle Gain (Beginner)
Slight surplus • strength training
Goal: Muscle gain (slight surplus) Breakfast: Oats + milk + banana + nuts Lunch: Rice + dhal + chicken/fish + veggies Snack: Peanut butter toast / curd / nuts Dinner: Protein + carbs + vegetables Water: 2–3L • Strength: 3–4 days/week
Maintenance (Busy Worker)
Balanced plate • simple habits
Goal: Maintain weight & energy Breakfast: Eggs/oats + fruit Lunch: 1/2 plate veggies + 1/4 protein + 1/4 carbs Snack: Fruit / yogurt / nuts Dinner: Light meal + protein Water: 2L • Steps: 6k–8k • Sleep: 7h

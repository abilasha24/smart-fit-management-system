Frequently Asked Questions
How long does it take to get a response to my feedback?
We aim to respond to all feedback within 24 hours. Bug reports and high priority issues are typically addressed within 4-6 hours during business hours. Questions and suggestions may take up to 48 hours for a detailed response.
Can I track the status of my feedback or bug report?
Yes! All your submitted feedback appears in your Feedback History with status updates. You'll see whether it's Pending, In Progress, or Closed. You'll also receive notifications when there are updates to your feedback.
What should I include in a bug report?
For effective bug reports, please include: 1) Steps to reproduce the issue, 2) Expected behavior, 3) Actual behavior, 4) Device and browser information, 5) Screenshots if possible, 6) The time when the issue occurred.
Do you implement user suggestions?
Absolutely! We regularly review user suggestions and many features in Smart Fit started as user suggestions. While we can't implement every suggestion, we prioritize based on user demand, feasibility, and alignment with our product vision. Popular suggestions are often added to our roadmap.
How can I follow up on my previous feedback?
You can reply to any feedback thread in your Feedback History. Our support team will see your follow-up and respond accordingly. You can also reference your feedback ID when contacting support through other channels.

About Smart Fit

Revolutionizing Fitness Learning in Sri Lanka

Our Story

Smart Fit was born from a simple observation: while fitness awareness is growing in Sri Lanka, access to professional guidance remains limited, especially outside urban areas.

Founded in 2024 as a B.Sc Software Engineering project, Smart Fit aims to bridge this gap by providing a comprehensive online platform that connects fitness enthusiasts with certified trainers.

Our mission is to make quality fitness education accessible, affordable, and effective for every Sri Lankan, regardless of their location or background.

Our Mission

To democratize fitness education in Sri Lanka by providing an accessible, affordable, and comprehensive online platform that empowers individuals to achieve their health and wellness goals regardless of their location or economic background.

Our Vision

To become Sri Lanka's leading digital fitness ecosystem, creating a healthier nation by 2030 through technology-enabled fitness education, community building, and personalized wellness solutions.
